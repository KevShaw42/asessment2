Rules of Rock, Paper, &Scissors

Objective

Rock, Paper, &Scissors is a two-player game that involves 3 possible "weapon" choices in order to defeat your opponent. Both players simultaneously pick a weapon to play against their opponent. Each choice has a strength and weakness to clearly define if it wins or loses based upon the opposite choice. If both opponents play the same weapon at the time, it is consisded a wash, and is repeated until a winner emerges.

Alternatives

There are games similar to Rock, Paper, &Scissors that involve the same 3-way relationship. A game called Ninja, Cowboy, &Bear. The biggest difference is not necessarily the rules, but the way the game is played.
